Rivalry network:

The size of the circles correspond to the number of tweets by the particular gang'g members.

mouseover: show rival/nonrival visit lines.

mouseout: hide rival/nonrival visit lines.

click: hold that gang's rival/nonrival visit lines.

Blue lines show rival territory visits, and Red lines show rival territory visits. The thickness of the lines correspond to the number of tweets in that visit.
